# Drop Dunks — Play Online

## About Drop Dunks

Bounce, aim, and score in Drop Dunks! Control a platform to deflect balls into baskets for points, while three dispensers challenge your reflexes. Strategically upgrade to new balls and cannons. It's basketball with a twist—where quick thinking and precision are your best teammates. Dive in and dominate the court!

## How to Play

1. Use arrow keys or WASD to move and Space to jump
2. Time your shots or dunks to score efficiently
3. Try fullscreen for an immersive experience

## Key Features

- Fast, fluid gameplay with responsive controls
- Clean visuals and readable court action
- Play instantly in your browser
