# Street Basketball — Play Online

## About Street Basketball

Master the thrill of street basketball in this intense one-on-one challenge. Outsmart opponents with precise movements, strategic fakes, and skillful shots. Enhance your player as you take on leagues and daring challenges. Your journey to street court stardom awaits—can you outplay the best and claim victory?

## How to Play

1. Use arrow keys or WASD to move and Space to jump
2. Time your shots or dunks to score efficiently
3. Try fullscreen for an immersive experience

- Fast, fluid gameplay with responsive controls
