## About Pixel Basket

Guide the ball through vibrant challenges in Pixel Basket! Skillfully draw a path around obstacles to sink the ball into the hoop. With increasing difficulty and a global leaderboard, this arcade adventure demands precision, clever planning, and a touch of competitive spirit. Can you master the maze?

## How to Play

1. Use arrow keys or WASD to move and Space to jump
2. Time your shots or dunks to score efficiently
3. Try fullscreen for an immersive experience

## Key Features

- Fast, fluid gameplay with responsive controls
- Clean visuals and readable court action
