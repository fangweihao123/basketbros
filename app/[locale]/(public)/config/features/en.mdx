# Basket Bros - Ultimate Basketball Gaming Experience

## What is Basket Bros?

Basket Bros is an exciting online basketball game featuring unique characters with special abilities. Experience fast-paced basketball action with stunning graphics and intuitive controls.

## How to Play

1. **Choose your favorite basketball character** - Select from various players with unique skills
2. **Use arrow keys to move and spacebar to jump** - Master the simple yet effective controls
3. **Dunk, shoot, and score to win the game!** - Show off your basketball skills

### Multiple Characters
Choose from various basketball players, each with unique abilities and special moves

### Smooth Gameplay
Experience fluid controls and responsive gameplay for the ultimate basketball experience

Play against friends or other players online in exciting multiplayer matches
